1. SELF JEWELRY COLLECTION BOX PREPARATION WORKSHOP

Show Details

Super User Activities September 20, 2022

A total of 20 staff members of the Terengganu State Museum participated in the Workshop for the Preparation of Personal Jewelry Collection Boxes located in the Telepok Room, Terengganu State Museum Complex. Encik Awang Ku Mohd Fadli bin Awang Besar, Assistant Curator of the Collection Management Division and Encik Mohamad Ruzi bin Sulaiman, the Senior Museum Assistant of the Collection Management Division from the Department of Museum Malaysia are the instructors who have been appointed to make the workshop a success.

This workshop is theoretically based and also practical regarding the making of ' custom-made ' boxes for a diverse collection, especially related to personal jewelry. The purpose of making this box is to protect the collection from being damaged or dusty. Among the collections produced to make ' custom-made ' boxes are bags, capes, dohohs, pendants, belts and anklets.

Congratulations to the line-up of instructors and participants of the Self Jewelry Collection Box Preparation Workshop.

2. COLLABORATION BETWEEN TERENGGANU STATE MUSEUM BOARD AND EAST COAST PETRONAS

Show Details

Super User Activities September 20, 2022

On Monday, 19 September 2022, a discussion regarding the Bidong Island cooperation project between the Terengganu State Museum Board (LMNT) and PETRONAS was held. The discussion was led by the Head of the Research and Documentation Sector, Mr Zakaria bin Abdul Hadi, LMNT and Mr Mohd Amin Demanis, Head of the Program Implementation Department from PETRONAS East Coast.
