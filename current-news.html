PLANNED EXPEDITION COLLECTING FOSSIL SPECIMENS

Super User | Current News | June 25, 2024

Planned Expedition Collecting Fossil Specimens

The Terengganu State Museum Board (LMNT) together with the Malaysian Department of Minerals and Geosciences, Terengganu (JMG) in the North Terengganu Integrated Agricultural Development Area (IADA KETARA), Paya Peda, Jerteh, Terengganu starting from 24 to 25 June 2024.

This program aims to collect, record, store and convey evidence of the natural history of the state of Terengganu in the past to the Terengganu community in particular.

In this second series program, we have managed to obtain important fossil samples of the existence of ancient flora in Terengganu around the location of Paya Peda which is millions of years old.
