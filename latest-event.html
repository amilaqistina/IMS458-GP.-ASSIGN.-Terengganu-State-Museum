RUST MARKET 2024

Super User | Latest Events | May 23, 2024

The 2024 rust market will take place throughout the school holidays. Starting on 25 May 2024 until 2 June 2024.

Located in the grounds of the Terengganu State Museum Board Complex from 9 am to 6 pm.

Among the sales held are, Sale of Antiques, Rusty Books, Handcrafted Goods, Bundled Goods, Traditional Food, Coffee Sale, and Rahmah Madani Sale.

There are also side activities throughout the Rust Market such as Serama Chicken Competition, Bonsai Competition, Relaxing Classic Moto, and many more.

AROUND THE OPENING CEREMONY OF THE ART, VISUAL & POETRY EXHIBITION OF STRUGGLE

Super User | Latest Events | February 29, 2024

Around the opening ceremony of the Art, Visual & Poetry Exhibition of Struggle: Palestine Solidarity and also the launch of the Department Activity Calendar 2024 which was inaugurated by YB Datuk Razali bin Idris, Chairman of the Terengganu Tourism, Culture, Environment & Climate Change Committee at Dewan Batu Bersurat on the morning of 29 February 2024.

The ceremony was further brightened up with poetry readings and the presence of Terengganu Painters (PeTer) and PELITA.

Thank you to YB Datuk and also the audience for enlivening the atmosphere.
